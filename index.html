Darcy Wilkin

A web developer from Kalamazoo, Michigan, Darcy is an experienced art teacher, which allows her to bring an artistic and critical eye to her work. When she's not programmimg and maintaining well-functioning web applications, she can often be found playing music with her band, the Corn Fed Girls. She's also co-host of the long-running Americana radio show, Grassroots, on WMUK, along with her dad and frequent musical collaborator, Mark Sahlgren.
